What is a QR code?

A QR (Quick Response) code is a square barcode that phones can scan with their camera to open a link, show text, or trigger an action. They're used on menus, posters, business cards, packaging, and event tickets. This generator turns any URL or text into a high-resolution QR code you can download and print.

Private by design

Your QR code is created entirely in your browser — the text you enter is never uploaded to any server. Download the PNG and use it anywhere, with no watermark and no expiry.
