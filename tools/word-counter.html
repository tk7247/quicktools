Why count words?

Word and character counts matter for essays with length requirements, articles, meta descriptions, tweets, and other posts with limits. This counter updates instantly as you type and also estimates reading time based on an average of 200 words per minute.

Private by design

Your text never leaves your device — all counting happens locally in your browser. Paste sensitive drafts without worry; nothing is uploaded or stored.
