How to convert hourly pay to salary

To convert an hourly wage to an annual salary, multiply the hourly rate by hours worked per week, then by 52 weeks. For example, $25/hour × 40 hours × 52 weeks = $52,000 per year. This calculator works in every direction — enter any pay period and it fills in the rest based on your weekly hours.

Gross vs. net

These figures are gross pay (before taxes and deductions). Your take-home pay will be lower depending on your tax situation, benefits, and location.
