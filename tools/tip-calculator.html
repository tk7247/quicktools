How to calculate a tip

To calculate a tip, multiply the bill amount by the tip percentage (as a decimal). For example, a 18% tip on a $54.20 bill is 54.20 × 0.18 = $9.76, for a total of $63.96. To split it, divide the total by the number of people. This calculator does it all automatically and updates instantly.

What is a standard tip?

In the United States, 15–20% is customary for sit-down restaurant service, with 18% a common default. For exceptional service many people tip 20–25%. This tool defaults to 18% but you can set any percentage.
