How to calculate a discount

Multiply the original price by the discount percentage to get the amount saved, then subtract it from the original price for the sale price. For example, 25% off $79.99 saves $20.00, for a sale price of $59.99. Add sales tax to the sale price for your true checkout total.

Quick mental math

For 10% off, move the decimal one place left. For 20% off, take 10% twice. For 25% off, divide by 4 and subtract. This calculator handles the exact figures — including tax — so you know what you'll actually pay.
