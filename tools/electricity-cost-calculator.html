How appliance running cost works

Electricity is billed per kilowatt-hour (kWh). An appliance's daily energy use equals its wattage × hours used ÷ 1000. Multiply by your price per kWh for the daily cost, then by 30 and 365 for monthly and yearly figures. High-wattage devices used for long periods — heaters, dryers, air conditioners — cost the most.
