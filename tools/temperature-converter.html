Temperature conversion formulas

To convert Celsius to Fahrenheit: °F = °C × 9/5 + 32. Fahrenheit to Celsius: °C = (°F − 32) × 5/9. Celsius to Kelvin: K = °C + 273.15. These formulas account for the different zero points and scale sizes of each system.
