How unit conversion works

Length and weight conversions work by expressing every unit relative to a base unit (meters for length, grams for weight), then scaling. Temperature is different — Celsius, Fahrenheit, and Kelvin use offset formulas rather than simple ratios. This tool handles all three correctly and updates as you type.

Common conversions

1 mile = 1.609 km, 1 foot = 0.3048 m, 1 kg = 2.205 lb, 1 ounce = 28.35 g. For temperature, °F = °C × 9/5 + 32, and Kelvin = °C + 273.15.
