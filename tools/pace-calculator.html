Understanding running pace

Pace is how long it takes to cover one unit of distance — usually shown as minutes per kilometer or minutes per mile. It's calculated by dividing your total time by the distance. Speed is the inverse: distance covered per hour. Runners use pace to plan races and pace themselves over long distances.

Plan your race

Enter a goal distance and target time to find the pace you need to hold. One mile equals about 1.609 kilometers, so a 5:00/km pace is roughly 8:03/mile.
