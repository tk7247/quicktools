How it's calculated

Your final grade is a weighted average of your current grade and your final exam. If the final is worth w percent of your grade, the score you need is: (target − current × (1 − w)) ÷ w. This tool does the math and tells you whether your target is still reachable.

What if the number is over 100?

If the required score is above 100%, your target isn't achievable from the final alone — consider extra credit or adjusting your goal. If it's below 0%, you've already secured your target no matter how the final goes.
