How GPA is calculated

Your grade point average is a weighted average: each grade is converted to points (A = 4.0, B = 3.0, and so on), multiplied by the course's credit hours, summed, and divided by total credit hours. Courses worth more credits therefore count more toward your GPA.

Grade scale used

This calculator uses the common US 4.0 scale with plus/minus grades (A− = 3.7, B+ = 3.3, etc.). Some schools weight honors or AP courses differently; check your institution's official policy for exact figures.
