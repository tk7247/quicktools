How to calculate percentages

X% of Y: multiply Y by X and divide by 100. X is what percent of Y: divide X by Y and multiply by 100. Percentage change: subtract the old value from the new value, divide by the old value, and multiply by 100 — a positive result is an increase, a negative result is a decrease.

Everyday uses

Percentages show up in discounts, tips, tax, grades, interest, and statistics. This calculator covers the three questions people search for most, so you can switch modes without re-learning the formula each time.
