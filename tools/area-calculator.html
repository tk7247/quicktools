How to calculate area

For a rectangle, multiply length by width. For a circle, multiply pi by the radius squared. For a triangle, multiply the base by the height and divide by two. The result is in square units — square feet or square meters — which is what you need for flooring, paint, turf, and landscaping.

Square footage for projects

Measure your room or area, enter the dimensions, and use the square footage to estimate how much material to buy. Add 5–10% extra for waste and cuts.
