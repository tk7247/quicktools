How to calculate hours worked

Subtract the start time from the end time to get total elapsed time, then subtract any unpaid breaks. If the end time is earlier than the start time, the shift is treated as crossing midnight (an overnight shift). Multiply the resulting hours by your hourly rate to estimate gross pay.

Time cards and shifts

This tool is handy for time cards, freelance billing, and shift planning. Results are shown both as decimal hours (for payroll) and in hours and minutes.
