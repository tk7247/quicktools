How age is calculated

Your age is the time elapsed between your date of birth and a target date (today by default). This tool counts complete years first, then the remaining months, then the leftover days — accounting for different month lengths and leap years — so the result matches how age is counted in everyday life.

Uses

Check exact age for forms, eligibility, milestones, or curiosity. Change the "age at date" field to find how old someone was (or will be) on any specific date.
