What's in a mortgage payment?

A typical monthly mortgage payment has four parts, often called PITI: Principal, Interest, Taxes, and Insurance. Principal and interest are calculated from your loan amount, rate, and term using the standard amortization formula; property tax and homeowners insurance are usually collected monthly and held in escrow.

Lower your payment

A larger down payment reduces the loan amount and may remove mortgage insurance. A lower rate or shorter term reduces total interest. Use this calculator to compare scenarios before you commit — it's an estimate, not a loan offer.
