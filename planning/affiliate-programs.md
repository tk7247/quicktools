Pick a few that match your tools, sign up (all free), grab your referral link, and send it to
me — I'll insert it into the matching tool pages' content blocks. You keep 100% of commissions.

> Honest notes: (1) commission figures change constantly — treat them as ballpark and confirm
> the real rate when you sign up. (2) Affiliate income needs visitors, so it grows as your
> search traffic does. (3) Some programs (esp. Amazon) require a few sales within a window to
> stay active. (4) Disclose affiliate links (a short "we may earn a commission" line) — it's
> required by the FTC and by most programs. I've left room for that on each page.
